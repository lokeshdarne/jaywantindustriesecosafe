Our Services

Biomedical Waste Collection & Transportation

We provide secure collection and safe transportation of all types of biomedical waste from healthcare facilities. Our services include:

  • Color-coded bins and bags for proper waste segregation
  • Customizable pickup schedules to meet your facility's needs
  • GPS-tracked transportation vehicles compliant with ISO standards

Advanced Treatment & Disposal

Our ISO 9001-certified treatment facilities employ multiple technologies to safely process different types of biomedical waste:

  • High-temperature incineration for pathological waste
  • Autoclaving for infectious materials
  • Chemical disinfection for liquid waste

Recycling & Resource Recovery

Our circular economy approach focuses on maximizing resource recovery while minimizing environmental impact:

  • Recovery of metals from treated medical equipment
  • Conversion of treated organic waste into energy
  • Diversion of 40% of processed materials from landfills

Regulatory Compliance & Staff Training

Our comprehensive compliance solutions help healthcare facilities meet all regulatory requirements:

  • Expert consulting on biomedical waste management regulations
  • Hands-on staff training for proper waste handling protocols
  • Documentation systems for audit-ready record keeping
